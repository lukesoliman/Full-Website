What is CAD?


CAD stands for Computer Aided Desgin. I use CAD to creat and desgin virtually anything. Athough there are small restrictions on what I can make in real life, I can make basically anything I desgin. I am very privalledge, because my school has a CNC I have access to, 3d printers, and many other power tools that is always open for my use. For CAD I use Fusion 360 mainly and SolidWorks sometimes. They both have different uses. Fusion is good for small projects because of its ease of use. I like Solid Works for bigger projects because, Fusion crashes sometimes, but SolidWorks can handle bigger files/projects.


New Note: I just started using Inventor and it is a better version of Fusion 360, I think I like it the best and I am going to continue using it to Judge if it is the best.
